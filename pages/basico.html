George Harrison

Dubbed "The Quiet Beatle," George Harrison shaped the sound of the Fab Four with his lead guitar playing, underrated songwriting, and embrace of Indian music and spirituality. In his post-Beatles solo career, Harrison notched two No. 1 albums -- including the 1971 landmark All Things Must Pass -- and scored five Top 10 hits on the Billboard Hot 100.
